SCHEDULE OPTIMIZATION ANALYSIS

CSV RULES:
- "s" = start week marker (exclusive)
- "e" = end week marker (exclusive)  
- Projects can shift between s and e weeks
- Projects can split hours across weeks (40h → 20h + 20h)
- People can swap if same role and improves fitness
- Total project hours never change
- Per person capacity: 40 hours/week

FITNESS SCORING (weighted):
- Conflict Score (40%): Penalize overallocation (>40h/week)
- Movement Score (20%): Penalize frequent project switching
- Focus Score (20%): Reward fewer simultaneous projects
- Continuity Score (10%): Reward longer consecutive weeks
- Duration Score (10%): Penalize very short assignments

TASK:
Compare ORIGINAL vs OPTIMIZED schedule and explain changes based on fitness rules.

OUTPUT:
1) SUMMARY (2-3 paragraphs)
   - What problems existed in original (conflicts, overallocation)
   - What changed in optimization
   - Why changes improve fitness score

2) KEY METRICS
   - Fitness score: before → after
   - Conflicts: count before → after
   - Rows changed: X

3) MAJOR CHANGES (top 3-5 most significant)
   - Person/Project/Week changes
   - Why each change improves fitness

4) REASONING
   - Which fitness rules drove the optimization
   - Trade-offs made (if any)

Keep explanations concise and evidence-based.
